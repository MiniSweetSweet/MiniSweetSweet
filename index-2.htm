About Us

REBEKAH comes from the Italian ceramic glaze heart production base, the Emilia-Romagna region. At the beginning of the last century, it provided exclusive ceramics customization services for the Italian royal aristocracy. It once led Italy's top fashion trends to the world. representative.

Today, REBEKAH inherits the spirit of historical ingenuity, and serves as the representative of the global high-end ceramic personalized custom brand with advanced design and international exemplary service market. In 2011, the REBEKAH Asia Pacific Executive Center was established in Hong Kong, mainly responsible for REBEKAH Asia Pacific import and export services; in 2015, the REBEKAH Greater China Operations Center was established in Foshan, focusing on the Chinese market and striving to bring Italian minimalist aesthetic space elements to China Chinese people's aesthetic concepts and consumption habits are transformed and upgraded, and they are at home with the international trend to create a quality life.

In the field of product development, REBEKAH cooperated with the Italian top original design company EGO DESIGN, inheriting the latest Italian design concepts, launched 70 ° natural light stone surface products in 2018, and launched the 7 series warm color products in the industry for the first time, triggering many domestic and foreign high-end customer groups attention. At present, REBEKAH is a strategic cooperation unit of Wenzhou Corning Medical Group (2120.HK) and a designated supplier of Huazhu Hotel Group (NASDAQ: HTHT).
